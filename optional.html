ResumeDraft Tech & Engineering Resumes

Contact details

This goes at the top of the resume. Name and email are the only must-haves — add the rest if you have it.

Summary

Optional. Two or three sentences on who you are and what you're looking for. Most tech resumes skip this and let experience speak — leave it blank if you'd rather.

Education

Most recent first. Include GPA only if it strengthens your application.

Work experience

Internships count. Start each bullet with an action verb and a result where you can — numbers help.

Projects

Especially useful if you're a student or early in your career. Link to GitHub or a live demo if you have one.

Technical skills

Group skills into categories. This is the section ATS scanners read closest — be specific and accurate.

Optional sections

Everything here is optional — leave anything blank and it just won't appear on your resume.

Review & download

Your resume is on the right, updating as you type. When it looks right, download it as a PDF.

Before you download

For tech & engineering roles, aim for one page if you have under ~5 years of experience. Check that dates are consistent and bullets lead with what you did and what changed because of it.
